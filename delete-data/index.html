Univera — Data Deletion Instructions

We respect your privacy. If you want Univera to delete your personal data (account, profile, favorites, messages, etc.), please follow one of the options below.

Option 1 — Request by email

Send an email to issacabhilash196@gmail.com with:

  • Your account email or UID (if known)
  • A short statement requesting deletion
We will reply and remove your data within 7 business days.

Option 2 — In-app / Website request

If available in-app, you can open Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will queue a deletion request that we will process within 7 business days.

What will we delete

  • Account information (name, email, profile picture)
  • User favorites and personal lists
  • Uploaded content associated with your account

Contact

If you do not get a response, contact us at issacabhilash196@gmail.com.
